Getting Started

We recommend working with our RSS Reader application. See this walkthrough on Answers For AWS to get up and running quickly. For the simple approach, try out our ZeroToDocker container images. After downloading the images, you can be up and running NetflixOSS in just a few minutes. After you've tackled that, check out the IBM ACME Air and Flux Capacitor apps, and the Zero-to-Cloud workshop. Also, be sure to join our mailing lists and follow us @NetflixOSS to stay up to date.

Cloud Platform Engineering

The cloud platform is the foundation and technology stack for the majority of the services within Netflix. The cloud platform consists of cloud services, application libraries and application containers. Specifically, the platform provides service discovery through Eureka, distributed configuration through Archaius, resilent and intelligent inter-process and service communication through Ribbon. The previous libraries and services can be used with any JVM based container. To help with consumption, the platform additionally provides JVM container services through Karyon and Governator and support for non-JVM runtimes via the Prana sidecar. Miscellaneous libraries that are part of the cloud platform include logging through Blitz4J, and .... Finally, in order to show how a JVM based Karyon application can be constructed, we provide the Recipes-RSS sample.
